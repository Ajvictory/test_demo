Variables are containers for storing data values

int: 		-127 to 127. This stands for integar
double:	-19.99 to 19.99. This stands for decimal.
char:		This stores single letter or character associated with single hyphen. [e.g 'a']
string:	This stores more than a letter or character associated with a double hyphen. [e.g "hello World"]
bool:		This means boolean and it carries 2 values: True or False.
